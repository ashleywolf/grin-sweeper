# Grin Sweeper 😁

Minesweeper, but you reveal tiles by smiling at your webcam. Wider smile = bigger reveal. Hit a mine and your manic grin gets immortalized as a "shame trophy."

## How to Play

Open in Chrome and allow webcam access. Hover your mouse over tiles, then smile to reveal them. The wider your grin, the more tiles you uncover at once. Right-click to flag suspected mines. You get 3 lives.

## Tech

MediaPipe FaceLandmarker (52 blendshapes), HTML5 Canvas. Single file, CDN-loaded, no build step.

## Input

Webcam (smile detection via `mouthSmileLeft`/`mouthSmileRight` blendshapes).
